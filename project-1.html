Project Overview

Navigation Bar: A header section with links to various sections of the website such as Home, About, Menu, Events, Gallery, and Contact. There is also a "Book a Table" button for reservations.
Hero Section: This section uses a carousel feature to display promotional slides showcasing the restaurant's key offerings. It highlights the restaurant’s Italian cuisine and invites users to explore the menu or book a table.
About Section: Provides information on the history and concept of Pasta Cosi. It emphasizes the restaurant's location in Tunis and offers details about the menu, including various types of pasta, risottos, and raclette. The section also includes a video that gives visitors an insight into the restaurant's environment and offerings.
Media Integration: The use of videos and high-quality images, such as a video in the About section and background images in the Hero section, enhances the website’s visual appeal and provides an immersive user experience.
Overall, the project combines smooth navigation with visual storytelling to effectively promote the restaurant's identity and attract potential customers to explore its menu and items.
